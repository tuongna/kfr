* Các cụm từ
Please speak slowly. – 천천히 말해 주세요.
Please say it again once more. – 다시 한번 말해 주세요.
What is it in English? – 영어로 뭐예요?
What is it in Korean? – 한국어로 뭐예요?
I can’t understand Korean well. – 한국어를 잘 못해요.
Hello / Good morning / Good afternoon / Good evening – 안녕하세요.
How have you been? – 잘 지냈어요?
Long time no see! – 오랜만이에요!
Goodbye (said by the person staying). – 안녕히 가세요.
Goodbye (said by the person leaving). – 안녕히 계세요.
Goodbye / Hello (friends only). – 안녕!
Yes. – 네.
No. – 아니요.
Maybe. – 아마도.
It’s okay. / I’m okay. – 괜찮아요.
No! (strong) / I can’t! – 안 돼요!
Thank you. – 감사합니다.
Thank you. – 고맙습니다.
Thanks (friends). – 고마워.
You’re welcome (not common). – 천만에요.
